Authorised IndiGo Cargo Agent Since 2018

PRADEEP CARGO & COURIER SERVICE is an authorised IndiGo Cargo agent based in Kolkata, providing airport-to-airport cargo movement and multi-modal freight coordination across India. Logistics companies trust us as their single-window partner — we route their shipments through the optimal medium (air via IndiGo Cargo, rail via Indian Railways, or road via interstate truck networks), handle all AWB / LR documentation, and track every consignment end-to-end. Our team's deep operational knowledge of airline cargo workflows, IndiGo's AWB system, railway freight scheduling and surface transport permits means you don't have to navigate the complexity alone.

Compliance

Dangerous Goods — Declared & Documented

Carrying undeclared dangerous goods by air is a serious offence under the Aircraft Act 1934 and the Carriage of Dangerous Goods Rules 2003. At PCCS, we pre-screen every consignment against IATA's Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) to ensure your shipment is fully compliant, properly declared and accepted by the carrier without delay.

What cannot travel undeclared

Flammable liquids & solids, compressed gases, lithium batteries above limits, oxidizers, toxic/infectious substances, radioactive material, corrosives and explosives. When in doubt, ask our cargo desk before booking — we'll classify, pack and document correctly.
